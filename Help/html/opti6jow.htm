Mail Server (Incoming POP/IMAP)

Specifies the name of your account on your incoming server (POP or IMAP) that delivers your email.

For example, if your assigned login name is "justine," and the name of the computer where you receive email is incoming.qualcomm.com type only incoming.qualcomm.com in this field. (When you enter your server name here, it is made available in the incoming Mail Server field of other Options categories.) Then your full email address is justine@incoming.qualcomm.com.

A POP account is an account on a computer that runs a Post Office Protocol version 3 (POP3 or POP) server. An IMAP account is an account on a computer that runs an Internet Message Access Protocol server. This is the account to which your email messages are delivered so that Eudora can find them and transfer them to your computer. You must have a POP or IMAP account to be able to receive mail with Eudora.
