Results List

Your query results are listed in the left half of the Directory Services window, in the results list (top view) and the details pane (bottom view). You can resize the two views relative to each other using the separator between them; drag the separator up or down.

The results list consists of one-line summaries of the successful targets of your query. These are listed by four columns: Name, Email, Phone, and Database. Name indicates the username (or other name) of the target. Email is the email address of the target. Phone is the target’s telephone number. Database tells you in which of your selected databases the target was found.
