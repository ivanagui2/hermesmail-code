Changing ESP Group Information

ESP allows you to change the configuration of a share group to meet the changing needs of its members. The same tabs (User, Eudora, Settings, Notifications, and Advanced) you used to create a share group and configure its attributes can be used to change those of an existing group.

To make changes to an existing share group, do the following:

1.From the Tools menu, choose ESP Groups. The ESP Groups window appears.

2.In the left column, choose the group you wish to change.

3.Click any of the tabs and make your changes in their respective dialog boxes.

4.When finished, click OK.
