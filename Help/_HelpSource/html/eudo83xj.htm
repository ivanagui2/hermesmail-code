Turbo Redirecting

You can redirect a message to someone on your recipient list, queue the new message (without displaying it), and delete the original message, all with one command. To turbo redirect a message, do the following:

1. From the Tools menu, choose Options.

2. Scroll and select the Miscellaneous icon. The Miscellaneous options window appears.

3. Check the Turbo redirect by default box and click OK.

When you select a recipient from the Redirect To submenu under the Message menu, a turbo redirect is automatically performed.
