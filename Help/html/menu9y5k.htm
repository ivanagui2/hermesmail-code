Trash (Mailbox menu)

Opens the Trash mailbox.

All deleted messages are stored in this mailbox until you select Empty Trash from the Special menu. If the Empty Trash when exiting option in the Miscellaneous Options is on, the Trash is automatically emptied when you exit Eudora. Deleting a message from this mailbox permanently deletes the message.

Note: Try not to keep too many messages in your Trash box because it can slow down your system’s performance. Try to empty your Trash box on a regular basis.
