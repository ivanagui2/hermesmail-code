Payment and Registration

To change modes of HERMES after you install HERMES, to pay for HERMES, and to register HERMES, do the following.

1.From the Help menu, choose Payment & Registration. The Payment and Registration dialog box appears.

2.In the Which HERMES is for you? section:

To use HERMES Email in sponsored mode, click Sponsored Mode (free, with ads). HERMES will display the ad window.

To use HERMES Email in paid mode, click Paid Mode (costs money, no ads). HERMES displays the HERMES web site for you to purchase HERMES.

To use HERMES Email in light mode, click Light Mode (free, fewer features). HERMES changes to HERMES Email in light mode by turning off certain features.

3.In the Keeping Current section:

To register HERMES, click Register with Us. HERMES takes you to a web site where you can register HERMES. It’s good to register because if you are using HERMES in paid mode, you will be eligible for technical support..

To customize the ads, click Customize the Ads You See. HERMES displays a web site where you can provide information about yourself to help in customizing which ads should be displayed to you.

To find and retrieve the most current version of HERMES, click Find the Latest Versions. The HERMES web site displays where you can retrieve the latest version of HERMES.

In the Your Registration Information section, click Change Your Registration to change your registration when you change from one HERMES mode to another. The Registration dialog box appears.

Enter the information and click OK.

If you cannot find your HERMES registration code, click I Lost the Code. The HERMES web site appears for you to search for your registration code.

4.To exit the window, click the X in the right-hand corner or the window.
