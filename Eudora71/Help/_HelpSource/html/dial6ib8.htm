Address Book Files and Entries

Lists all your Address Book files and entries.

Files are shown as book icons and can be opened and closed by double-clicking on them.

·To select multiple files and entries, hold down the Shift key and make selections in sequence, or hold down the Ctrl key and make selections out of sequence.
