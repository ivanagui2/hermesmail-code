Statistics Window

The statistics you see in this window keep track of how many messages you receive and send in a particular day, week, month, or year. You can also see how many attachments were received and sent as well as how many messages were read. In the Sent Email section, you can see how many messages you have replied to, forwarded, and redirected during a specific timeframe.

Note. Eudora usage statistics are statistics for you only. No statistical information is sent to Qualcomm or any other web site address.
