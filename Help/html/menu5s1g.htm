Out (Mailbox menu)

Opens the Out mailbox.

All messages you compose, save, and queue are stored in this mailbox until they are sent. Also, if the Keep copies option in the Sending Mail Options is on, a copy of each outgoing message is saved in this mailbox.

Note: Try not to keep too many messages in your Out box because it can slow down your system’s performance. However, you can transfer messages from your Out box to other mailboxes.
